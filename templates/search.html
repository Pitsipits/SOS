1. Before a long drive, always get enough sleep and eat a snack or meal.
2. Before setting off, be sure your vehicle is in prime condition
3. Do not drive when drunk
4. Pull over and take breaks every couple of hours
5. If you can, share the driving responsibilities with someone else.
6. Pay attention to traffic signs.
7. Avoid using phone while driving
8. Having an emergency kit before heading on a road trip is essential
